On a more technical note, an index doesn ºt have to be an integer, nor does it have to be unique. For example, we can set the
index of the elections DataFrame to be the name of presidential candidates.
# Notice how our indices, or row labels, are the same
s_a = pd.Series(["a1", "a2", "a3"], index = ["r1", "r2", "r3"])
s_b = pd.Series(["b1", "b2", "b3"], index = ["r1", "r2", "r3"])
pd.DataFrame(s_a)
s_b.to_frame()
pd.DataFrame({
    "A-column": s_a, 
    "B-column": s_b
})
