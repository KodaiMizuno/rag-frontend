2.4 Parting Note
The pandas library is enormous and contains many useful functions. Here is a link to its documentation. We certainly donʼt
expect you to memorize each and every method of the library, and we will give you a reference sheet for exams.
The introductory Data 100 pandas lectures will provide a high-level view of the key data structures and methods that will form
the foundation of your pandas knowledge. A goal of this course is to help you build your familiarity with the real-world
programming practice of … Googling! Answers to your questions can be found in documentation, Stack Overflow, etc. Being
able to search for, read, and implement documentation is an important life skill for any data scientist.
