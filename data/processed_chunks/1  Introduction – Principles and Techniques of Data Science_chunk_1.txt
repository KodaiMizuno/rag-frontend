Data science is an interdisciplinary field with a variety of applications and offers great potential to address
challenging societal issues. By building data science skills, you can empower yourself to participate in and drive
conversations that shape your life and society as a whole, whether that be fighting against climate change,
launching diversity initiatives, or more.
The field of data science is rapidly evolving; many of the key technical underpinnings in modern-day data science
have been popularized during the early 21  century, and you will learn them throughout the course. It has a wide
range of applications from science and medicine to sports.
