Notice how the first argument to both .loc and .iloc are the same. This is because the row with a label of 0 is conveniently
in the  (equivalently, the first position) of the elections DataFrame. Generally, this is true of any DataFrame where the
row labels are incremented in ascending order from 0.
And, as before, if we were to pass in only one single value argument, our result would be a Series.
